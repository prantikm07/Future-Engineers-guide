Welcome Future Engineers to First Year Guidebook!
This website is your ultimate resource hub for first-year students, thoughtfully curated to support your academic journey. Here, you’ll find a comprehensive collection of theory chapters paired with relevant YouTube video links, making learning more accessible and engaging. Whether you're diving into core subjects or exploring non-core branches, our resources cover essential topics such as Mathematics, Chemistry, Physics, Programming for Problem Solving, Basic Electrical and Electronics Engineering, and English Language and Technical Communication. We aim to empower you with the tools and knowledge you need to thrive in your first year. Explore, learn, and excel with the First Year Guide!
